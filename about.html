About Us

Welcome to Grameen Shiksha, a social impact initiative born out of a hackathon project. Our mission is to bridge the educational infrastructure gap in rural India by leveraging software solutions. We aim to provide remote learning opportunities, optimize resource management, and improve connectivity for students in rural areas. Our team is passionate about creating inclusive and accessible education for all, and we believe that technology can play a vital role in achieving this goal. Through our innovative solutions, we strive to enhance the quality of education and make a positive impact on the lives of rural students. Join us in our journey to empower rural education and create a brighter future for all.
